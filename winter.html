Darragh McCreery

writer
Darker roads in cold winter light
 Pave the way to home hearth,
 Heart, hares late in the field
 Some littered, mixi-afflicted
 Bodies twist, stiff and decaying
 Growing from the splintered shin
 Bulbous spores dark brown and capped
 Life from life in spring,
 Life in death is winters claim.
